ABOUT US

GREAT FOOD COMES FIRST

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit Zaid Burger Place restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are known for serving high quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 1954, Zaid Burger Place is the second largest fast food hamburger chain in the world. Our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and family friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 50 successful years.

Transformation

In 2010, 3G Capital, a global multi million dollar investment firm focused on long term value creation, purchased Burger King Corporation, making it a privately held company.
